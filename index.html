Welcome to FilesFound!

I'm PIXELATEDCROWN, and I've picked up a fun hobby:
Looking through obsolete data storage for digital treasure!

Floppy disks, VHS tapes, MiniDiscs, CDs, game cartridges -
I love to look through them all for anything interesting.

I wanted to make a website where I could share and write about the things that I find...

And so, FilesFound! was born!
